Welcome to MES Golden Jubilee College Kottayam, a milestone of academic excellence and extracurricular performances which has enlarged and enriched Literary City Kottayam's educational map. As we embark into the 9th year of incorporation, our commitment at MES to provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers and inspired learners prepared to thrive in the 21st century.

In 11 years of its reign, MES has imparted quality education, strong & dynamic entrepreneurs have evolved out of this institute. High standards and expectations for each student in regard to academic performance, co-curricular participation and responsible citizenship are the foundation of our institute.

It is with pride that we hold these high standards and ask each of our students to commit to maintaining the extraordinary record of achievement and contribution that has been the legacy of MES students for the past 11 years.
