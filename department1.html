Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA - 3 Year Degree Program

The Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.) is a 3 year degree course, designed to provide a clear understanding of the business oriented concepts. The objective of BBA course is to build competence in students to effectively undertake entry level managerial jobs in organisations and to develop various soft skills required for the profession. BBA course allows a student to enter the diverse field of finance and management. The program also serves as a stepping-stone for higher career programs like MBA, CA, ICWA, CS, ICFA etc.

Eligibility & Course Details

Eligibility for Admission

Applicants are required to have passed Plus Two or equivalent examination with not less than 50% marks in aggregate.

Course Duration

3 Year Course (Total 6 Semesters)

Merit Calculation

Marks obtained in Part III of plus two or equivalent less handicap marks with 50% quota for science group plus weightage mark if any.

Bachelor of Computer Applications

BCA - 3 Year Degree Program

The Department of Computer Application offers a 3-year degree programme in Computer Applications (BCA). Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate program where students are exposed to various areas of Computer Applications including the latest developments in the industry. Department has well qualified and vibrant faculty dedicated to train the students. Most of the students passed out are well placed or have opted for higher studies. The department has a well-equipped computer lab with latest computers, software and high speed internet. The depar-tment ensures that program objectives are constantly met and learning outcomes are monitored through periodical tests and assignments. Every year, on an average 60% of the students are getting placement through various campus recruitments. The department encourages students to get funded projects and we have a set of completed and on-going projects. The department also undertakes website developm-ent as consultancy service.

Eligibility & Course Details

Eligibility for Admission

Candidates shall be required to have passed the Plus Two/equivalent examination in science stream with Mathematics/Computer Science/Informatics as optional.

Course Duration

3 Year Course (Total 6 Semesters)

Merit Calculation

Total Marks obtained in Part III less handicap marks obtained for Maths/Computer Science Plus Weightage mark if any.

"Technology Embedded Training for Students" is intended to provide short term training with long-term assistance in IT related streams. TETS aims to improve requisite skills and knowledge to understand key concepts that can be applied to IT.
